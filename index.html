To · Rose Garg
My Dearest Rose,
There are words I have rehearsed a thousand times in the quiet of my own mind, and yet each time I try to say them to you, they dissolve into something softer — something that feels more like a feeling than a sentence.

You are not the kind of person the world simply notices. You are the kind of person the world remembers. The way you laugh, freely and without apology. The way your eyes hold an entire story before your lips even part. The way you love the roar of an engine and the softness of a strawberry shake in the same breath — that is your magic, Rose. You are a beautiful contradiction, and I am endlessly in awe of you.

I do not need a grand occasion to tell you this. I tell you now, on an ordinary day, because ordinary days with you are the most extraordinary thing I have ever known.

I am yours — completely, quietly, joyfully — yours.
Forever Yours 🌹

Why I Love You
There are infinite reasons. Here are just a few.
01
The way you make a room feel different
The moment you walk in, something shifts. The air feels warmer. Everything feels more alive. You do not try — you simply are.
02
Your love for speed & freedom
The way your eyes light up at a roaring engine, the way you love the feeling of going fast — it mirrors how brave and free your spirit truly is.
03
How beautifully soft you are inside
Behind the bold exterior is a girl who loves strawberry shakes, mango ice cream and vanilla custard. Your softness is one of the most beautiful things about you.
04
Your effortless elegance
You carry yourself with a grace that feels both ancient and modern — like a woman painted into a European masterpiece, yet entirely, uniquely yourself.
05
The colours you see the world in
Pastel pinks, butter yellows, emerald greens, the depth of maroon — your palette tells the story of someone who feels the world deeply and beautifully.
06
The way you are my favourite thought
In every quiet moment, in every loud one, you are the thought I return to. Not because I choose to — but because my heart already knows where home is.
07
Simply because you are Rose
There is no better reason. No more complete answer. You, exactly as you are, is the reason. You are enough. You are everything.
& a thousand more
This list could never end.
Because loving you has no end.
